About

Evgeniy Moroz Colorist Based in Kyiv, Ukraine

I'm Evgeniy Moroz, a colorist working across commercials, music videos and fashion films.

Color, for me, is an extension of direction. I shape light, contrast and palette so a frame holds attention and carries the right mood — from restrained minimalism to rich, cinematic looks.

I work in DaVinci Resolve with footage of any format — from ARRI and RED to drones and mobile cameras. I handle full final grades as well as look development and pre-production tests.
